The gist of q1 is :
Courses:
1. Get a TA from select list of labs
2. Allocate a random number of seats b/w 1 and max_seats
3. Take a tutorial
4. Repeat until TAs are unavailable
Students
1. Apply for course
2. Get a seat
3. After tutorial decide to either repeat same steps witha diff course or exit simulation

TAs/Labs
Basically a resource, A TA can be only used a fixed number of times as specified by the lab it belongs to
